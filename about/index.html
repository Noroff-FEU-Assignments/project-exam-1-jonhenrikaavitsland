About Keeper Evans

Welcome to "The Zookeeper's Journal"

Meet Keeper Evans, the heart and soul of our extraordinary zoo.

At the age of 29, Keeper Evans is not just your average zookeeper; he's a guardian of a realm where reality seamlessly intertwines with fantasy.

His passion for the inhabitants of this magical zoo knows no bounds, and he has embarked on a journey to unveil the secrets that unfold when the park gates close and the world becomes exclusively theirs.
